Account Deletion Request

MilliVee Clothing

Delete Your Account and Data

We respect your right to privacy and data control. You can request deletion of your MilliVee Clothing account and associated data at any time.

⚠️ Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Please make sure you want to proceed before submitting your request.

How to Request Account Deletion

Send an email to our privacy team with your deletion request:

📧 Send Deletion Request Email

Email address: privacy@millivee.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Include in your email:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your registered name
  • Confirmation that you want to delete your account
  • Reason for deletion (optional)
💡 Quick Tip: Click the button above to automatically fill in the email template, or manually send an email with the required information.

What Gets Deleted

Data Type Action
Personal Information ✅ Deleted (name, email, phone, address)
Profile Data ✅ Deleted (profile picture, preferences)
Authentication ✅ Deleted (login credentials)
Wishlist ✅ Deleted
Cart Items ✅ Deleted
Product Reviews ⚠️ Anonymized (kept for product ratings)
Order History ⏳ Retained (see below)
📋 Order History Retention: Due to legal and accounting requirements, order history and transaction records are retained for 7 years as required by Ghanaian law. However, your personal identifiers are removed, and the data is anonymized.

Data Retention Period

Immediate Deletion (within 24 hours):

  • Account access (you can't log in)
  • Personal profile information
  • Preferences and settings
  • Wishlist and saved items

Deleted within 30 days:

  • All personal data from our systems
  • Cached data from backups
  • Third-party service data (Firebase, Cloudinary)

Retained (anonymized) for 7 years:

  • Order transaction records (required by law)
  • Anonymized analytics data
  • Financial records for accounting

Before You Delete

Please consider the following before deleting your account:

  • ❌ You will lose access to your order history
  • ❌ Active orders cannot be tracked after deletion
  • ❌ Wishlist items will be permanently lost
  • ❌ Earned rewards or credits will be forfeited
  • ❌ You cannot recover your account after deletion

Alternative: Deactivate Account

If you're not ready for permanent deletion, you can request account deactivation instead. This allows you to return later and reactivate.

Contact us at support@millivee.com for account deactivation.

Processing Time

  • Email requests: Processed within 3-5 business days
  • Confirmation: You'll receive an email when deletion is complete

Canceling a Deletion Request

If you change your mind, you can cancel your deletion request within 48 hours by emailing us at privacy@millivee.com.

Contact Information

Your Rights

  • ✅ Request deletion of your personal data
  • ✅ Access your personal data
  • ✅ Correct inaccurate data
  • ✅ Export your data
  • ✅ Object to data processing
  • ✅ Withdraw consent at any time
